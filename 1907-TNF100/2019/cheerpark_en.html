Shuo-Ren Elementary School

Cheering Station: Shuo-Ren Elementary School/ at 32km/ Participants group: 50K and 100K

In 1940, SanDiaLing recruited many students because of the booming coal mining industry. At the time, Shuo-Ren Elementary School was the only elementary school in SanDiaLing.

However, Shuo-Ren Elementary School was abandoned and has been like that for more than 30 years and most of the school buildings are still intact. It is a great chance to experience the history of coal mining while cheering for friends and family.

Hou-Tong Eco-Environmental Education Park

Hou-Tong Eco-Environmental Education Park at 40&95km/Participants group: 50K and 100K

A ruined elementary school reborn – Houtong (Monkey Cave) Ecological Park

The great damage caused by Typhoon Xangsane back in 2000 destroyed and ruined the old campus of Houtong Elementary School. It was not until 2011 that, after years of the place being abandoned, reconstrucction works began and the "Houtong (Monkey Cave) Ecological Park" was established.

The vast diversity of plants allow visitors to inmerse themselves into nature and forget the worries of living in the city. It is with this idea that we chose this place for friends and relatives to wait and support all the athletes! Let the natural beauty of this area lift your spirit and of those running through it!

Human Space Café next to Wanggu station

Cheering Station: Human Space Café next to Wanggu station /At 63.5km/ Participants group: 50K

Wanggu station is a small station on the Pingxi line. The desolate trail features a quiet area perfect to enjoy the scenery and nature. Human Space Café is right next to Wanggu station where heavy motorbikes and bikes usually stop for a drink. There are various vintage heavy motorbikes on display and it is a perfect place to wait for the runners to come.
