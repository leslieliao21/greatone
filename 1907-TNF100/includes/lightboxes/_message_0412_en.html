Implementation of Alternate Routes:

The race organiser will decide to implement new routes in the case of the following conditions:

  1. Continuous heavy rain for more than 2 days before the race day or extreme rainfall one day before the race (accumulated rainfall is more than 80mm in the last 24 hours, or hourly rainfall is more than 40mm)

  2. Continuous rainfall (2.6mm-8mm per hour) during the event ( Apr.20 to 21. )

*Note: If an alternate route is implemented for the 20KM group the ITRA points will not be obtained.
